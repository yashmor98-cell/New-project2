FAQ

Clear answers before you start.

Here are the questions visa applicants usually ask before choosing a documentation and consultation service.

Can you guarantee visa approval?

No. Visa decisions are made only by the relevant embassy, consulate, immigration department, or visa authority. Our service helps you prepare a clear, complete, and consistent application file.

Which countries do you support?

We support visa preparation for Europe Schengen destinations, Russia, Australia, the United Kingdom, New Zealand, and Canada.

Do you help first-time travelers?

Yes. First-time applicants often need extra help with travel purpose, financial proof, itinerary, employment documents, and home-ties evidence.

How early should I start?

Start as early as possible, especially for destinations with appointment availability issues or peak travel seasons. We can also help with urgent document readiness where timelines allow.

What documents will I need?

Documents depend on your destination, visa type, employment status, travel purpose, sponsorship, and personal history. After consultation, we prepare a customized checklist.

Can you fill out the visa form for me?

We provide form preparation support and consistency checks using the information you provide. You remain responsible for the truth and accuracy of all submitted information.

Do you arrange travel insurance or flight bookings?

We can guide what proof is commonly required and review documents you arrange. Any third-party booking or insurance purchase should be selected carefully based on your destination rules.

Can you help after a refusal?

Yes. We can review the refusal reason, identify weak areas, and help plan a stronger reapplication where appropriate.

Is my information kept private?

Yes. Visa applications involve sensitive personal, financial, and family details, so privacy and careful document handling are core to the service.
